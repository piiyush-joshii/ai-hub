SECTION 5 — PAYMENT TERMS
================================================================================

Payment Due          : Net 30 days from date of valid invoice.
                       Invoices must be submitted by the 7th calendar day
                       of the month following the service month.
                       Late invoice submission (after the 7th) shifts the
                       payment due date to Net 30 from the actual
                       submission date, not the service month end.

Cash Application
  Window             : Buyer's accounts payable team processes facilities
                       invoices in two payment runs per month:
                         Run 1 — 15th of each month (covers invoices
                                 received by the 10th)
                         Run 2 — Last business day of month (covers invoices
                                 received between 11th and 25th)
                       Invoices received after the 25th are held for
                       Run 1 of the following month.
                       Cash Reconciliation Agent must use these windows
                       to determine expected cash outflow dates.

Early Payment
  Discount           : None applicable.
Late Payment
  Penalty            : 1.25% per month on balances outstanding beyond
                       Net 30. Accrues from Day 31 onward.
Reconciliation Cycle : Monthly close. Buyer's Cash Reconciliation Agent
                       reconciles expected vs actual payments on the last
                       business day of each month. Supplier must provide
                       a remittance summary by the 28th of each month
                       showing all open invoices, expected payment dates,
                       and any credits or adjustments.

Payment Method       : ACH to Supplier's designated US bank account.
                       Payment reference must include contract number
                       C-2022-008 and the service month (e.g. MAY-2026).

SECTION 7 — LATE SUPPLIER CLASSIFICATION
================================================================================

For purposes of Cash Reconciliation Agent classification:

  LATE_NORMAL        : Supplier has submitted invoice or remittance late
                       in 3 or more of the last 6 months — consistent
                       pattern, do not escalate automatically.
  LATE_ANOMALY       : Supplier is typically on time but submits late
                       in the current month — escalate to Exception Agent.
  CHRONICALLY_LATE   : Supplier late in 5 or more of the last 6 months —
                       flag for contract review; Buyer may invoke cure notice.
