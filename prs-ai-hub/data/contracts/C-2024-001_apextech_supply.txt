================================================================================

                       committed window.

                       5th business day of each month covering the prior
                       month's fill rate, on-time delivery, and any open
                       backorders. Late or missing reports trigger an
                       automatic escalation to the Exception Resolution Agent.

                       falls below 90% in any single month, Buyer may apply
                       a service credit of 2% of that month's invoice value.
                       Two consecutive months of breach entitle Buyer to
                       issue a formal cure notice.
